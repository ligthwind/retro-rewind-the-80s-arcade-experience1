# 🕹️ Project: Retro Rewind – The 80s Arcade Experience

Welcome to your next AP Computer Science Principles project! Step into a time machine and travel back to the 1980s—the "Golden Age" of video games. 

The 1980s gave us the rise of arcade cabinets at the mall, home consoles like the NES and Atari 2600, and legendary franchises like *Pac-Man*, *Donkey Kong*, *Super Mario Bros.*, *The Legend of Zelda*, and *Tetris*. Your mission is to design and develop a browser-based, 80s-inspired arcade game using JavaScript.

## 👾 The Vibe
80s games were defined by **high scores, fast action, and simple but highly addictive mechanics**. You don't need complex 3D worlds; you need a game that is fun, challenging, and makes the player want to insert another quarter.

## 🛠️ Technical Requirements

- **Platform:** Browser-based game programmed in JavaScript.
- **Engine:** Use the **p5play** or **q5play** library to handle physics, collisions, and sprites.
- **Game States:** Your game must include a functional game loop:
  1. `Title/Start Screen`
  2. `Active Gameplay`
  3. `Game Over / Score Screen`
- **Mechanics:** Implement clear rules, a working scoring system, and a defined win/loss condition.

## 🎨 Art & Assets

- You must use 2D sprites for your game characters, enemies, and interactive objects. 
- You can utilize free, open-source sprite sheets from available libraries, or create your own custom pixel art. 
- **Recommended Tool:** Check out [PiskelApp](https://www.piskelapp.com/) to easily draw and animate your own retro sprites right in your browser.

## 📝 The "Inspiration" Write-Up

Along with your code repository, you must submit a short paragraph (3-5 sentences) detailing your inspiration. You must explicitly name the 1980s game(s) that inspired your project and explain how you translated its mechanics or theme into your own original creation. 

> **Need Inspiration?** Check out the [Top 25 80s Video Games (IMDb)](https://www.imdb.com/list/ls044827639/) or browse articles and emulated games at [Free80sArcade.com](https://www.free80sarcade.com/).
